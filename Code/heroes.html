Iron Man

Tony Stark

I am Iron Man

Tony Stark created the Iron Man suit to escape captivity in an Afghan cave. With his superior intellect, he was able to escape and eventually use his AI JARVIS to perfect a working Iron Man suit. The suit gives him super strength and durability.

Captain America

Steve Rogers

I can do this all day

Steve Rogers was given powers through the Super-Soldier Serum in the 1940s. He gained increased strength, speed, stamina, and agility. He also possesses a vibranium shield.

Thor/God of Thunder

Thor Odinson

He's a friend from work

Thor is from the realm of Asgard where the people there possess superhuman strength and longevity. He can summon his trusty hammer, Mjolnir, which also grants him the power of flight. Later on, he can summon Stormbreaker as well as Mjolnir.

Hulk

Bruce Banner

That's my secret, Cap: I'm always angry

Bruce Banner has a genius-level intelligence (with 7 PhDs to prove it). He was exposed to gamma radiation which turned him into Hulk. He can change into the Hulk at will and in doing so, has the abilities of long-distance jumping, superhuman strength, and regeneration.

Black Widow

Natasha Romanoff

I'm sorry. Did I step on your moment?

Natasha Romanoff was forced into training to become a spy/assassin in Russia. She is a highly skilled fighter with finely-tuned reflexes and sharp instincts. She also has a strong knowledge of computer systems with an ability to hack into just about anything for information.

Hawkeye

Clint Barton

Just can't seem to miss.

Clint is a master archer and marksman.

Agent Fury

Nick Fury

The last time I trusted someone, I lost an eye

Nick Fury was at one point the director of SHIELD. He helped found the Avengers Initiative and actively recruited individuals for the Avengers. He is an experienced leader and an expert in hand-to-hand combat.

War Machine

Colonel James "Rhodey" Rhodes

What's up regular sized man?

Colonel Rhodes is a skilled fighter and possesses the powers of the War Machine suit. It is comparable to the Iron Man suit as both were designed by Tony Stark. His suit allows him to fly and contains repulsors.

Falcon

Sam Wilson

I don’t know if you’ve been in a fight before but there’s usually not this much talking.

Sam Wilson is a former US. Air force pilot who manned the EXO-7 Falcon suit. He is a skilled fighter and the suit gives him the ability to fly. He also has a reconnaissance drone, Redwing.

The Winter Soldier

James Buchanan "Bucky" Barnes

I'm with you 'til the end of the line.

Bucky Barnes fought alongside Steve Rogers in World War II. After his fall off of a train, he was taken by HYDRA to become a brain-washed assassin. He possesses a metal arm, amplified skills in hand-to-hand combat, and a high accuracy with knives.
